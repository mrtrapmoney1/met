How Our Service Works

As a factory-authorized servicer, our process is designed to work seamlessly with your manufacturer's warranty.

For Warranty Appliance & TV Repair

1. Contact Your Manufacturer: Start by calling the warranty department for your brand (e.g., Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool).

2. Open a Service Claim: Provide them with your model number, serial number, and proof of purchase to open a new service claim.

3. Request Metro TV & Appliance: Tell them you want 'Metro TV & Appliance' in Lincoln, NE as your authorized servicer.

4. We Contact You: The manufacturer will send us the work order, and we will call you to schedule your in-home appointment.

For Audio Equipment Repair

Our passion for electronics started with audio. We are one of the few shops that still service high-fidelity and vintage audio equipment.

For out-of-warranty repairs on items like vintage receivers, turntables, or high-end audio gear, you can contact us directly.
