Why Our Electronics Heritage Matters

Today's appliances are 70% electronics. Our 1947 roots in TV and audio repair give us a unique advantage.

From TV Circuit Boards to Smart Appliances

When we opened in 1947, we were fixing the complex circuitry inside televisions—the most advanced consumer electronics of the era. That same expertise now applies to modern appliances.

Today's refrigerators have more computing power than the Apollo 11 spacecraft. Your washer uses sensors, inverter boards, and microprocessors. Your oven has touchscreen controls and WiFi connectivity.

While others see appliances, we see the electronics inside. That's our advantage.

How Our Service Works

As a factory-authorized servicer, our process is designed to work seamlessly with your manufacturer's warranty.

For Warranty Appliance & TV Repair

1. Contact Your Manufacturer: Start by calling the warranty department for your brand (e.g., Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool).

2. Open a Service Claim: Provide them with your model number, serial number, and proof of purchase to open a new service claim.

3. Request Metro TV & Appliance: Tell them you want 'Metro TV & Appliance' in Lincoln, NE as your authorized servicer.

4. We Contact You: The manufacturer will send us the work order, and we will call you to schedule your in-home appointment.
