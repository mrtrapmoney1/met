Our Story: From 1947 to Today

1947

The Beginning: Post-War Electronics Boom

As America emerged from World War II, returning servicemen brought home technical knowledge gained from military electronics training. In this era of optimism and innovation, our founder opened Metro TV's doors in Lincoln, Nebraska, specializing in the most advanced consumer technology of the day: television repair.

In 1947, television was cutting-edge. Only a few thousand American households owned a TV set, and repairing the complex vacuum tube circuitry required genuine expertise. We were there at the beginning, building our reputation one repair at a time.

1950-1989

Four Decades of Electronic Expertise

As television evolved from black-and-white to color, from tubes to transistors to integrated circuits, we evolved with it. We repaired everything from 1950s console TVs to 1980s VCRs, building deep knowledge of electronic systems that few repair shops could match.

Lincoln families came to trust us because we didn't just replace parts—we understood how electronics worked. That philosophy remains our foundation today.

1995

The Audio Tech Merger

In the mid-1990s, we merged with Audio Tech, Lincoln's premier high-fidelity audio repair specialist. This partnership brought together two complementary skill sets: our television electronics expertise and Audio Tech's mastery of stereo systems, receivers, amplifiers, and turntables.

The merger expanded our capabilities and deepened our commitment to quality electronics service. We became Metro TV & Appliance, positioning ourselves for the digital age while maintaining our passion for vintage audio equipment that audiophiles cherish.

2005

The Appliance Revolution

As the new millennium arrived, we recognized a fundamental shift: appliances were becoming computers. Refrigerators, washers, ovens, and dishwashers were suddenly packed with microprocessors, circuit boards, sensors, and digital controls.

Other appliance repair shops struggled with the electronics. We thrived. Our decades of experience diagnosing complex circuitry gave us a unique advantage. We expanded into appliance repair, quickly earning factory authorizations from major manufacturers who recognized our electronic expertise.

Today

A Legacy of Excellence

Today, we are Nebraska's premier factory-authorized service partner for 11+ major brands, from Samsung smart refrigerators to LG ThinQ appliances to vintage McIntosh audio equipment. We serve commercial accounts with priority scheduling and residential customers with the same care we've provided since 1947.

Three generations of Lincoln families have trusted us. We're honored by that legacy—and committed to earning your trust for generations to come.

75+ Years. One Mission: Expert, Honest Service.
