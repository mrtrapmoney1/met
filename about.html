Our Story

Our story begins in 1947, with a passion for the latest electronics. As televisions became a household staple, Metro TV grew as Lincoln's go-to specialist for expert service. In the 1990s, we merged with Audio Tech to expand our expertise in high-fidelity sound.

As the new millennium began, we saw the future: appliances were becoming computers. Refrigerators, washers, and ovens were suddenly filled with microprocessors and complex circuit boards. We knew our deep experience in electronics was more critical than ever. We expanded into appliance repair, applying our specialist knowledge to the entire home.

Today, we are proud to be Nebraska's premier factory-authorized service partner for the world's biggest brands. We've grown by listening to our neighbors, and we're committed to being your all-in-one repair partner for years to come.
