How Appliance Warranties Work

What's Covered

Most manufacturer warranties cover defects in materials and workmanship for 1 year from purchase date. Extended warranties may cover longer periods.

✓ Typically Covered:

  • • Parts failures due to defects
  • • Labor for authorized repairs
  • • Functional component failures
  • • Manufacturing defects

✗ Typically NOT Covered:

  • • Cosmetic damage
  • • User error or misuse
  • • Normal wear and tear
  • • Damage from improper installation
  • • Non-authorized repairs (voids warranty)

Getting Warranty Service - Step by Step

1

Locate Your Model & Serial Number

Find on appliance rating plate. Refrigerators: inside wall. Washers/dryers: inside door frame. Ovens: inside door edge.

2

Contact Your Manufacturer

Call the brand's warranty phone number. Have model, serial, purchase date, and proof of purchase ready.

3

Request Metro TV & Appliance

Tell the manufacturer: "I want Metro TV & Appliance in Lincoln, NE" - they'll send us the work order.

4

We Contact You Within 24 Hours

Once we receive the manufacturer work order, we'll call to schedule your in-home diagnostic appointment.

5

Service & Repair

Our technician diagnoses, orders genuine OEM parts through MSA, completes repair, and handles all warranty documentation.

Warranty FAQ

How do I know if I'm still under warranty?

Most appliances have 1-year parts and labor warranties from purchase date. Check your receipt or contact the manufacturer with your model/serial number.

What if I lost my receipt?

Manufacturers can sometimes verify purchase date through serial number. Credit card statements or delivery records may also serve as proof of purchase.

Can I choose my own repair service?

Yes! For warranty repairs, tell your manufacturer you want Metro TV & Appliance. They'll dispatch us directly.

What about out-of-warranty repairs?

Contact us directly at (402) 466-9090 for out-of-warranty service. We still use genuine OEM parts through our MSA partnership.
