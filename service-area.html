    Service Area FAQ

    Do you serve my area?

    If your location is within 50 miles of Lincoln, we likely serve it. Call (402) 466-9090 to confirm coverage for your specific address.

    Do you charge travel fees?

    No travel fees within Lincoln city limits. Extended service areas may have minimal trip charges—we'll quote before scheduling.

    What if I'm outside your service area?

    Call us anyway—we may be able to accommodate, especially for commercial accounts or specialized repairs. We can also provide referrals to authorized service centers in other regions.
