Why Does "Factory-Authorized" Matter?

When your appliance is under warranty, the manufacturer will only pay for repairs done by an authorized service center. But it's more than just a warranty requirement.

  • Exclusive Training: Our technicians receive direct, ongoing training from brands like Samsung, Electrolux, and GE on their latest models and technologies.
  • Proprietary Tools: We have access to the special diagnostic software and tools that non-authorized shops don't. This lets us pinpoint the problem faster and more accurately.
  • Genuine Parts: We use only genuine, factory-spec parts, ensuring your repair is durable and reliable.
  • Keeps Your Warranty Intact: Using an unauthorized repairer can void your manufacturer's warranty, even if the repair is unrelated to the original problem.

Choosing a factory-authorized servicer is the only way to guarantee your product is fixed to the manufacturer's standard.
