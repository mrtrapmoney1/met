For Warranty Appliance & TV Repair

Service must be initiated through your manufacturer.

As a factory-authorized servicer, we are dispatched by the manufacturer. Please contact your brand's warranty support line first.

1. Call Your Manufacturer (e.g., Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool) to open a service claim.

2. Request Metro TV & Appliance as your authorized servicer.

3. We Will Call You once we receive the work order to schedule your appointment.

For Audio Equipment Repair

For vintage audio or out-of-warranty systems, please contact us directly.

Bring your unit to our shop or call us at the number below for a consultation.
